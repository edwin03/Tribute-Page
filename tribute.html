Herder Kaldi (Discovered Coffee)

Legends say Herder Kaldi discovered the coffee plant

How Herder Kaldi discovered coffee timeline:

  • 850 AD: 00 - Kaldi, an Ethiopian, was herding his goat as any other normal day.
  • 850 AD: 01 - While herding his goats, he notice his goats were chewing on red berries.
  • 850 AD: 02 - Notice that his goats went from normal to jumping goats.
  • 850 AD: 03 - Kaldi tried it himself and notice the energy boost he got from them.
  • 850 AD: 04 - His excitement at what he found out he took it a nearby monk for approval.
  • 850 AD: 05 - The monk disapproved of their use and threw them to a nearby file, which then produced a pleasant aroma.
  • 850 AD: 06 - Then they raked the embers from it and dissolved in water to make the world's first coffee.
